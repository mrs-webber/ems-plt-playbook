### Agenda

| Time | Focus |
| --- | --- |
| 0–5 min | Review student data patterns. |
| 5–20 min | Identify which instructional strategies appeared to work best. |
| 20–30 min | Discuss differences in results without blame. |
| 30–40 min | Decide what the team should replicate, revise, or stop doing. |
| 40–45 min | Capture teacher learning before planning intervention. |

### Why This Meeting Matters

This meeting protects the PLC process from becoming only about student deficits. The question is not just "Which students didn't learn?" It is also "What did we learn as teachers?"
