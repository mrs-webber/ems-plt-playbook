### Agenda

| Time | Focus |
| --- | --- |
| 0–5 min | Revisit the selected essential standard. |
| 5–15 min | Identify key concepts, skills, vocabulary, and rigor. |
| 15–35 min | Break the standard into learning targets. |
| 35–43 min | Draft student-friendly "I can" statements. |
| 43–45 min | Decide which learning targets are most important to assess. |

### Why This Meeting Matters

Teams often think they agree on a standard until they start defining what proficiency actually looks like. This meeting creates shared clarity.
