### Agenda

| Time | Focus |
| --- | --- |
| 0–5 min | Review team norms. |
| 5–15 min | Complete a team self-assessment. |
| 15–30 min | Discuss patterns: Where are we already functioning well? Where are we stuck? |
| 30–40 min | Identify one area of focus for the first learning cycle. |
| 40–45 min | Choose the course/unit area the team will use for this year's cycle. |

### Why This Meeting Matters

Teams cannot improve what they have not named. This meeting gives teams permission to be honest without judgment.
