> **Buffer meeting.** No new content — this is a reinforcement and correction meeting. The first intentional pause built into the year.

### Agenda

| Time | Focus |
| --- | --- |
| 0–10 min | Revisit any confusion from Meetings 1–3. |
| 10–20 min | Review team norms and roles. |
| 20–30 min | Confirm the course/unit focus for the learning cycle. |
| 30–40 min | Identify possible essential standards for that unit. |
| 40–45 min | Decide what standards/materials everyone needs to bring next time. |

### Why This Meeting Matters

This is the first intentional buffer. Some teams will still be confused, skeptical, or waiting to be told exactly what to do. This meeting prevents teams from moving forward without clarity.
